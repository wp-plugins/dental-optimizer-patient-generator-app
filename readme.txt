This is a simple WP Plugin which lets you create and display "Dental Optimizer Patient Generator App" on Pages!

== Description ==

The Patient Generator widget routes prospective patients who visit your website to contact you based on their dental needs and proximity to your practice.

This application can be embedded in your website, social media pages, even on your Dental Optimizer profile. It encourages your visitors or prospective patients to ask questions, take dental evaluation assessments and get a cost estimate. As visitors interact they're directed to contact you for an appointment. As they fill out the assessments, you will have background information on their needs before they come in. 

This plugin access remote website [DentalOptimizer](http://www.dentaloptimizer.com "DentalOptimizer") to display dental widget as a web service on your website by accessing necessary data, js files and css files.

This plugin also provides facility to login or register on [DentalOptimizer](http://www.dentaloptimizer.com "DentalOptimizer"). It sends credentials and accesses your account on [DentalOptimizer](http://www.dentaloptimizer.com "DentalOptimizer").

The Oral Health Profile gauges the current state of the user's teeth based on symptoms they're experiencing as well as their day-to-day habits. Participating users are encouraged to make an appointment and you receive this information when they do.

The Dental Cost Calculator allows the user to receive an estimate for the costs of the services they think they might need. Your use of this particular feature is highly customizable so that you won't misrepresent your practice while the user doesn't get unnecessary sticker-shock.

Your own edition of the Patient Generator can be customized with the features you prefer. Of course, the Patient Generator is mobile optimized so users can easily use it from their smart phones and tablets.


== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Login into admin section.
2. Go to **Plugins** > **Add New** > **Upload**, upload dental-patient-generator-app.zip and activate it    or   Upload 'dental-patient-generator-app' folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory and Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to **Dental Widget** in left section.
4. Login using "Dental Optimizer" account username and password Or signup to create account into "Dental Optimizer", We send these credentials to [DentalOptimizer](http://www.dentaloptimizer.com "DentalOptimizer") to authenticate or register.
5. Select the modules, and theme to create widget.
6. Use html code to display "Dental Optimizer" widget on any html page or in post 
	or
  use shortcode [ps-dental-widget-code] in post and widget
        or
  Go to **Appearance** > **Widgets**  put "Dental Widget" in sidebar to display widget in sidebar on pages. 
7. For more information watch [youtube http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WdF8TeodAdw&feature=youtu.be]

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Dental Optimizer Patient Generator App? =

The Dental Optimizer Patient Generator App is an application that can be installed as a widget or embedded within a page, allowing you to more easily and frequently get your patients and prospective patients to contact you and schedule appointments. It's easy to install and easier to use.

= Is the Patient Generator limited to my Dental Optimizer profile? =

Absolutely not. You can embed it in your website, blog, social media pages, and more.

= It says the Patient Generator is powered by the Dental Optimizer. What is that? =

The Dental Optimizer is a resource that connects patients and dentists using some of the same tools as the Patient Generator combined with extensive search engine optimization. You can get a free Dental Optimizer profile at [DentalOptimizer](http://www.dentaloptimizer.com/dentists/dental-web-marketing/ "Dental Web Marketing")  but it is not necessary to use the Patient Generator.

= Do I need to log in every day to monitor my contacts? =

No. We'll send you an email when someone has a dental question, wishes to share their risk assessment results or oral profile, or requests an estimate for a specific procedure.
